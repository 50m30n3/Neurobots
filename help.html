Tips

  • The brain options only take effect on newly added bots.
  • Smaller brain size and complexity speeds up evolution but also limits the bots potential.
  • Be aware that even with small brains it can take a really long time to see any development.
  • You can control the view using your middle mouse button.
  • Click on bots to view their stats and export their brain.
  • To import a new brain select a bot, paste the brain data into the text box and press the "Inject!" button.
  • You can use the "Save" button to save brain data locally.
  • Increase the food respawn delay to force your bots to become better at finding food.
